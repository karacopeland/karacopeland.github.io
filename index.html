About Me

I am 21 years old and a graphic designer from Cincinnati, OH. Much of the work I have done so far has been free lance and school projects where I study at the AIC: College of Design. Among many other things for my future I intend to put myself out in the world and show everyone who I am mentally as well as emotionally.In 2016 I will be graduating from AIC: College of Design with my Bachelor's Degree for the Sciences of Graphic Design. I expect to find a job in graphic design with an emphasis on fashion.
